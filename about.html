About Bus Trade Center

We buy, and sell buses.

Since 1997, Bus Trade Center has helped over 20,000 Buses find new homes by bringing together owners and sellers in a simple and efficient way. In our database, you who are looking for a bus can quickly find the right vehicle for you and also add guards for objects that we do not currently have for sale.

Our warehouse vehicles are located at the parking lots in Järfälla and Kungsängen.

We offer, among other things:

  • Purchase of buses, regardless of age and condition, as long as they are drivable.
  • Sales assignment without requirements for exclusive rights with free marketing in Sweden and Europe's largest advertising markets.
  • Agreed car deals in the future - in case it would be preferable for you.
  • Flexibility and accommodating when it comes to finding flexible ways to sell buse.
  • Free surveillance & search buses according to the customer's specification and needs.
